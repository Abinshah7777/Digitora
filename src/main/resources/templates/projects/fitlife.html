Project Overview

FitLife is a cross-platform mobile application designed to help users track workouts, nutrition, and fitness goals with intelligent AI-powered recommendations. The app provides a seamless experience on both iOS and Android with real-time synchronization and personalized coaching.

Challenge: The fitness app market is crowded. We needed to create an app that stands out through intelligent insights, ease of use, and genuine value. The app had to work offline, sync seamlessly, and provide actionable recommendations.
